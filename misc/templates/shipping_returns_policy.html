Shipping & Returns Policy

JJuice Shipping Policy

  • JJuice LLC is committed to providing the highest quality vaping supplies and equipment. Our goal is to provide kickass customer service.
  • JJuice LLC will ship orders within 2 business days of receipt. Orders placed after 12PM MST on a Friday will ship on the following Monday.
  • JJuice offers FREE SHIPPING on any order over $100.00
  • Shipping charges are determined at the time of the order. JJuice LLC guarantees that there will be no additional charges related to shipping added to the order when released to the carrier. Flat rate of $2.95 for any order under $100.00.

Customers will be able to view the status of their shipment by going to http://usps.com and entering the Tracking Number that will be provided in the Shipment Confirmation Email that will be sent to the email address provided at the time of the order.

JJuice Refund Policy

In order to start a replacement or refund, request a return merchandise authorization number (RMA) by sending an email to support@vapejjuice.com. Include your name, shipping address, daytime telephone number, and order number. A return email with your RMA number and instructions for return will be sent to the email address you provided. Shipping costs for returning products to JJuice LLC are the responsibility of the buyer. If you have questions, contact James at (909) 767-8551. Refund Policy: If you are dissatisfied with the products you receive, you may request a refund. Within 14 days of your purchase, request a RMA number (listed in the steps above), once receiving the damaged goods at our offices, we will ship a replacement to you within 2-5 business days.
